Bang! AMD started 2019 with something of a bang.

At the beginning of 2019, AMD had a lot to respond to. The best and worst Intel Core i7 Ice Lake laptops of 2019

Read this before buying your next laptop
April 7, 2019

Dont miss! Despite its turbulent development history, Intel managed to push out the 10 nm Ice Lake series of ULV processors before year's end. Actual Ultrabooks sporting the fancy new CPUs, however, have been limited to just a small handful. We've tested six different laptops all with the Core i7-1065G7 CPU to see which of these early Ice Lake units you should consider as well which ones you should avoid from a pure performance point-of-view. The current top two laptops that represent Ice lake at its best are the Dell XPS 13 7390 2-in-1 and 15-inch Microsoft Surface Laptop 3. As shown by our benchmarks below, the Core i7 in the Dell convertible benefits from faster Turbo Boost than every other Ice Lake laptop even though the advantage is temporary. Clock rates eventually fall and stabilize at 3.0 GHz with a very warm CPU temperature of 97 C. The Microsoft laptop is able to outperform the Dell by 8 percent over longer periods according to CineBench R15 Multi-Thread and at a much lower running temperature of 77 C.
